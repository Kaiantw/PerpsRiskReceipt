# financial risk guardrails

## sources checked

- SEC/CFTC investor bulletin on Bitcoin futures: https://www.investor.gov/introduction-investing/general-resources/news-alerts/alerts-bulletins/investor-bulletins/funds-trading-bitcoin-futures-investor-bulletin
- SEC margin account bulletin: https://www.sec.gov/investor/alerts/ib_marginaccounts.pdf
- FINRA crypto asset risk page: https://www.finra.org/investors/investing/investment-products/crypto-assets/risks

## takeaways

- Futures and crypto exposure are high-risk and can involve rapid losses,
  volatility, and market-structure risks.
- Margin increases exposure and can create larger losses, additional collateral
  pressure, and forced liquidation behavior.
- A product assistant should explain what the loaded metrics mean, cite the
  snapshot fields it used, and refuse recommendations about opening, closing,
  increasing, reducing, or levering positions.
